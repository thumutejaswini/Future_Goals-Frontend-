Home Science Courses

Students aspiring to learn about food and nutrition, resources management, fabric and apparel sciences, human development and extension education can pursue Home Science Courses after 10th. It is a branch of science revolving around the study of general topics which arise on socio-economic level.

Certification and Diploma in Home Science Courses can be pursued just after completion of 10th. Admissions to these Courses are done on the basis of merit or on a first come first serve basis. However, admission to Bachelor's, Master's or PhD courses may be through entrance exams depending on the Institute.

Scope of Home Science Courses After 10th is immense. Candidates can pursue a career as Nutritionist, Dietitian, Human Resource Generalist, Assistant Human Resource Manager,etc. The average annual salary in this field ranges between INR 2,00,000 - 7,00,000 per annum.

Home Science Courses After 10th: Admission

  • The eligibility criteria of the course depends on various factors.
  • Admissions to Certificate and Diploma Courses are done on merit basis. Students can visit the Institute's website and apply.
  • After filling up the required details, submit the application form with provision of application fee.
  • Wait for further notifications from the Institute, if selected you can be admitted accordingly.
  • Admissions for Bachelor's and Master's may be through merit or entrance based.
  • After application of the form, candidates need to pass the entrance exams to get admission, wait for the Institute to release minimum qualifying marks.
  • Only after securing more than the qualifying marks, can a candidate get admission.
