Diploma in Electrical Engineering Course: Overview

What is Diploma in Electrical Engineering?

Diploma in Electrical Engineering is a three-year junior engineering program also known as a polytechnic in electrical engineering. It deals with the study of electricity and its applications, some other terms are electronics, electromagnetism, electrical devices, etc. It is one of the most popular electrical courses after 10th

It is a skill-based technical course in which you will get both theoretical as well as practical knowledge. In the diploma in Electrical course, you will learn a lot about electricity such as how electricity works, the uses of electricity, how electricity is made, current, types of current, voltage, circuits, power, transformers, generator, types of generators, electric motor, types of electric motor, electric devices, electric machines, and many more.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering after 12th (Lateral Entry)

If you have passed in 12th standard then you can take direct admission in the 2nd year of the Diploma in Electrical Engineering course. For that, you have to qualify for the entrance exam which is commonly called as Lateral Entry Polytechnic Examination in a majority of the state in India. The state education board conducts the Lateral Entry Polytechnic Examination. The selection process is based on the performance in the entrance exam.

The majority of the polytechnic colleges in India accept admission through lateral entry. However, the percentage of seats in the lateral entry quota is less than the general entry quota. Also, You should have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics subjects in 10+2. Basically, you should be 12th pass from the science stream.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering Eligibility

Candidates who want to do a diploma in electrical engineering course from a polytechnic college must full fill these below eligibility criteria. The eligibility criteria for a diploma in electrical engineering course are as follow:

  • Candidates must have passed in 10th standard or equivalent from a recognized board of education.
  • Candidates should be passed 10th from the science discipline.
  • Candidates should also be admitted after 12th.
  • Candidates should take physics, chemistry, and mathematics subjects in 10+2.
  • Candidates should get at least 40% marks in their respective examinations.
  • Candidates should minimum of 15 years old at the time of admission.

    • Diploma in Electrical Engineering Duration

    The duration of the diploma in electrical engineering course after 10th is 3 years and after 12th is 2 years. There are 2 semesters in each year and the duration of each semester is 6 months. So, If you are doing a Diploma in Electrical Engineering course after 10th then there will be a total of 6 semesters for you and if you are doing it after 12th then there will be 4 semesters.

    Diploma in Electrical Engineering Admission 2022

    Generally, admission to diploma in an electrical engineering course in any government polytechnic college is done by conducting entrance examinations but there are many polytechnic colleges that take direct admission or on the basis of merit lists. So, if you want to take admission to government polytechnic colleges then you have to qualify for the entrance examination which is conducted by the state education board authorities.

    Direct Admission

    In the direct admission process, candidates have to fill out the admission application form along with their documents which are necessary at the time of admission.

    Merit-Based Admission

    In merit-based admission, the selection process is based on the merit list which is released by the authorities. Firstly, candidates should apply by filling out the admission form with documents. After a couple of days, the authorities will release the merit list.

    Entrance Exam Based

    In entrance-based admission, the selection process of candidates has based on the results that candidates get in the exam. Every state education board in India conducts a polytechnic entrance exam which you can give after 10th class.
