DIGITAL MARKETING: AN INTRODUCTION

First of all, let us check out the definition of Digital Marketing –

Digital Marketing deals with marketing products and services using digital technologies.

Traditional marketing uses conventional methods to promote and market products and services. Digital Marketing uses digital technologies (internet, mobile phones, digital advertising and other forms of digital medium) to market products and services.

Digital Marketing is like an umbrella. Under this umbrella, there exists components like SEO (Search Engine Optimization), SEM (Search Engine Marketing), SMM (Social Media Marketing), Web Designing, Mobile Marketing, E Mail Marketing, E Commerce Marketing, SMO (Social Media Optimization) and Data Analytics.

In short, digital marketing is a vast and diverse field. It is made up of modules and components that are related to and/or influence each other (directly or indirectly). Also check – Data entry operator course.

While traditional marketing used to be effective in the past, those old methods are not very efficient in this digital age! “What are the uses of digital marketing?”, you may ask. The primary use of digital marketing is to promote and market products and services across various digital medium.

Businesses (big, small and medium sized), brands, individuals – anyone can use digital marketing to reach out to their audience and promote products or services. Digital Marketing can also be used to improve customer service, customer engagement and brand reputation.

    Benefits of doing digital marketing course after 10th

    There are several benefits to pursuing a digital marketing course after 10th standard, including:

    1.Career Opportunities:

    Digital marketing is a rapidly growing industry and there is a high demand for professionals with the skills to plan and execute effective digital marketing campaigns. By completing a digital marketing course, you can gain the skills and knowledge needed to pursue a career in this field.

    2.Enhance Employability:

    In today's job market, employers are increasingly seeking candidates with digital marketing skills. By completing a digital marketing course, you can demonstrate to potential employers that you have the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in a digital marketing role.

    3.Hands-on Experience:

    Digital marketing courses often include practical assignments and projects that provide hands-on experience with real-world digital marketing scenarios. This experience can be valuable when it comes to securing a job in the field.

    4.Networking Opportunities:

    Digital marketing courses often bring together students with similar interests and career goals. This provides an opportunity to network with other individuals in the industry, as well as potential employers and business contacts.

    5.Stay Ahead of the Curve

    The digital marketing industry is constantly evolving, and it's important to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices. By completing a digital marketing course, you can ensure that you are staying ahead of the curve and have the latest knowledge and skills needed to succeed in this field.

    6.Self-Employment Opportunities:

    Digital marketing skills can also be applied to start your own digital marketing business, offering services such as search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, email marketing, and more.


    Overall, a digital marketing course after 10th standard can provide a wealth of benefits, including new career opportunities, enhanced employability, hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and the chance to stay ahead of the curve in the rapidly evolving digital marketing industry.
