Diploma in Web Designing

The Diploma in Web Designing is a 1–2-year long course which allows students to learn about the various aspects of designing a website. The admissions in this course are offered on the basis of personal interviews and qualifying marks in the respective entrance examinations. The starting salary after the completion of the diploma is INR 2-8 lakhs per annum. Web application developers, layout analysts, web marketing analysts, front end developers, etc are the ones who must pursue this course. The blog provides you with all the information related to the Diploma in Web Designing course.

What is the Diploma in Web Designing?

The Diploma in Web Designing is a great course to pursue in today’s times due to this being a dominant sector in the IT industry. The growing influence of websites on the minds of customers has caused the biggest companies to seek out the best web designers.     • Eligibility Requirements for Diploma in Web Designing

    Although the eligibility criteria to pursue the Diploma in Web Designing course varies from university to university, given below is a general eligibility criterion for aspirants to refer to in order to pursue this course:

  • The candidate must have graduated from a recognised university with a bachelor’s degree in a relevant field.
  • Candidates must have received a minimum of 50-55% in their undergraduate.
  • Candidates must also meet the admission requirements for the college to which they wish to apply.
  • Some colleges and universities have their own entrance exams that applicants must pass.
  • If you want to study abroad, you’ll need to take an English proficiency test like the IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE.
