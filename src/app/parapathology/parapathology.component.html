Pathology Lab Technician Course: BSc Medical Lab Technology

The main objective of this pathology lab technician course is to instruct students on the various testing techniques that are used to carry out tests accurately, including urine testing, DNA typing, and other analysis. The topic of the course is how to diagnose a problem using a clinical laboratory. Graduates of BSc MLT degree programs have numerous choices for employment in a variety of industries. An MLT alumnus can pursue a challenging job in a public or private clinical setting, such as in blood donation facilities, ERs, labs. Eligibility: This pathology lab technician course qualification is a minimum of 50% marks in class 12, with PCB as the subject combination. Duration: It is a 4-year course. Institutes: Galgotias University- Gurugram, Sushant University- Gurugram.

Pathology Lab Technician Course: Bachelor’s in Medical Laboratory Technology (BMLT)

The DMLT course teaches students how to identify, and examine cells, bodily fluids, and tissues, help in the diagnosis, manage, and prevent diseases by using many methodologies and laboratory equipment. This course is intended to give students a thorough understanding of the fields of chemistry, microbiology, histology, and hematology. A functioning medical facility needs an MLT as the analyses carried out by the laboratory technicians provide the clinicians with the skills they need for proper diagnosis and treatment. Eligibility: This pathology lab technician course qualification is a minimum of 50% marks in class 12, with PCB as the subject combination. Duration: It is a 2-year course. Institutes: JIPMER- Puducherry, GGSIPU- New Delhi, SRM- Chennai.
